This is a modular system that can be extended with additional apps and utilities.

# Available apps

All this plugins are Open Source and available in the [Elo Workbench](https://github.com/elocuency/elo-workbench) repository.

## elo-obsidian-core-plugin

This is an Obsidian plugin that allows you to create your personal vault more easily using AI utilities and templates services.

The main idea is that you keep a daily journal of your experiences, the people you meet, and the places you visit. With these plugins, it is very easy to add information from known sources, while templates with prompts allow you to easily create and link new information to your vault.

## elo-obsidian-google-maps-plugin

This is an Obsidian plugin that allows you to search for places in Google Maps and add them to your vault.

## elo-obsidian-google-contacts-plugin

This is an Obsidian plugin that allows you to search for contacts in Google Contacts and add them to your vault to be linked.

## elo-obsidian-spotify-plugin

This is an Obsidian plugin that allows you to search for songs on Spotify, add them to your vault, and listen to them within your vault.

## elo-obsidian-quiz-plugin

This is an Obsidian plugin that allows you to speak about information in your notes and generate a quiz based on that information.

## elo-mac-bridge

This is a Mac app that allows you to retrieve information from your Mac and send it to your vault. This was necessary to access photos and contacts from the Mac. It acts as a bridge for anything you require from your Mac and handles special security access.

It was created for the `elo-obsidian-mac-bridge-plugin`, but it can be used for other purposes.

## elo-obsidian-mac-bridge-plugin

This is an Obsidian plugin that allows you to retrieve information from your Mac and send it to your vault. This was necessary to access photos and contacts from the Mac. It connects with the `elo-mac-bridge` app to retrieve the information.
